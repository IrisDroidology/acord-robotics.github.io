Unfortunately Essendon lost the game against Fremantle last Sunday by 37 points, but there was definitely some young and old talent emerging. Zaharakis, my favourite player, had 19 possesions and a few clearances and inside 50's. We led until the fourth quarter and didn't kick a goal in the last 20 minutes. But I was happy with our performance and stayed until the final siren holding my banner up and starting the chants.&nbsp;

We play Geelong next week and we should be able to be competitive.&nbsp;
