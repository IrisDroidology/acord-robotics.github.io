<span style="color: black; font-family: Times New Roman; font-size: 13pt;">Make sure you change the BOARDtype to match your board (in my case I use a SparkFun Pro Micro, I'd have to change it to 2). If you have one of them, change the PSItype to the correct one. 2400 is the right baud rate to connect to the current (v.1.5) version of the MarcDuino HP Controller, so you can leave that alone.

<span style="color: black; font-family: Times New Roman; font-size: 13pt;">Next you will need to upload your code. Next, if you use a FTDI programmer, go to the Tools-&gt;Serial Port menu and make sure you select the COM port to which your FTDI programmer has attached itself. The click on the green circle arrow pointing to the right to compile and upload your code. The Teeces lights should come on right after the upload is finished.
